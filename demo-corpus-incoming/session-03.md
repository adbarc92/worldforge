# Session 3 — The Harvest-Tide Murder

**In-world date:** 14th of Harvest-Tide, 847
**Location:** Hollowford, the ducal apartments

Duke Aldric was murdered in his chambers during the harvest festival. The party found him at the second bell of the evening — slumped at his writing desk, throat cut, still warm. The wound was a single drawn cut, right-handed, by someone the duke had allowed to stand behind him.

Duke Aldric's body was discovered by Corrin, who had slipped past the guard rotation and found the interior door to the duke's study ajar. The sealed letter the party had carried from Thornwatch was open on the desk, its wax seal heated rather than broken by force. A silver pendant of Fenland make lay on the floor half under the desk. The window latch was open from the inside.

Sister Maren performed last rites and raised the alarm. The party were held four hours for questioning, then released on the intercession of Lady Elayne's chamberlain. By the time they were shown out, the sealed letter was missing.

## Loose Threads

- Who let the killer into Duke Aldric's corridor?
- The window latch was open from the inside — for whom?
- Why was the Fenland pendant left behind?
