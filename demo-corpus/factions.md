# Factions of the Silverflow Valley

## The Crown of Eastmarch

The king's administration, seated at Hollowford. King Aelwin II has spent most of his reign consolidating authority that drifted to the duchies during the Fenland Incursions. Treats the provincial nobility with polite mistrust. Funds the Road Wardens and the Ashen Guard directly from the royal treasury.

**Goals:** Centralization. Control of the river trade. A quiet succession when the king's health finally gives.
**Attitude toward the party:** Correct, distant, transactional. They are useful so long as they do not embarrass the crown.
**Key figures:** Lord Chancellor Miraste, Captain-Commander Yvelle of the Ashen Guard.

## The Council of Stewards

A standing body of senior administrators — some hereditary, some appointed — that manages duchy affairs during stewardship, succession disputes, or the absence of a ruling duke. In theory advisory. In practice, the council has real teeth: it controls the duchy seal and the granary ledgers during any interregnum.

**Goals:** Institutional continuity. Protect the prerogatives of the stewardship office against crown encroachment.
**Attitude toward the party:** Watchful. Will support them only if their actions can be framed as protecting the office.
**Key figures:** Steward-Principal Callum Orell, Scribe-Warden Bess Tallow.

## House Vareth (Westvale)

The ducal house itself. Currently fractured: Lady Elayne is the heir-apparent but has not been formally confirmed. Her half-brother Edric is the son of Duke Aldric's first marriage and has a thinner but existing claim. Extended kin hold most of the Westvale castellanies and are watching to see which way the confirmation goes.

**Goals:** Varies by branch. The Greystone branch (Elayne's) wants confirmation. The Thornwatch branch (Edric's) wants delay.
**Attitude toward the party:** Elayne's faction is warming. Edric's faction regards the party as outsiders.

## The Fenland Envoys

A diplomatic legation from the Fenlands, officially in Hollowford to renew the Greystone Ford trade accords. Small — perhaps fifteen people including retinue. Led by Ambassador Suneth Vae. Their presence has been extended twice without clear reason.

**Goals:** Publicly, accord renewal. Privately, unknown. The Fenland pendant found near the duke is either theirs or forged to look theirs.
**Attitude toward the party:** Wary. Ambassador Suneth has not yet acknowledged the party exists.

## The Mendicant Order of Saint Perell

A pacifist clerical order that maintains shrines along the southern villages. Duke Aldric was their longstanding patron. They have no political ambition but do have a network: every village shrine-keeper writes monthly to the mother house at Halebrook.

**Goals:** Preserve patronage. Protect the rural shrines during any succession turbulence.
**Attitude toward the party:** Friendly if approached respectfully. Sister Maren is a lay affiliate.

## The Silverflow Guild

The merchant consortium that runs river traffic between Hollowford and the Isles. Wealthy, untitled, pragmatic. They answer to the money and the money is currently nervous.

**Goals:** Stable succession, open river, no inspection regimes.
**Attitude toward the party:** Will buy information. Will sell it too.
