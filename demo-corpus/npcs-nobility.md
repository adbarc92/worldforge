# NPCs — Nobility and Court Figures

## Royal Household

### King Aelwin II
Mid-fifties, reserved, quietly ill for longer than the court admits. Holds morning audiences three days a week. Speaks softly; courtiers lean in. Has no surviving children; succession runs through his sister's line.

### Princess-Sister Maelith
The king's younger sister and presumptive regent if the king's health fails. Commands more day-to-day loyalty among the Ashen Guard than the king himself. Polite to strangers, impossible to read.

### Lord Chancellor Miraste
Thirties, academic background, moved up fast under Aelwin. Carries the royal seal. Keeps a private ledger of favors owed, which is said to be longer than the Silverflow.

## House Vareth of Westvale

### Duke Aldric Vareth (deceased)
Late fifties when he died. Tall, narrow build, a falconer's calluses on his left hand. Kept a fixed annual schedule: summers at Greystone, autumn at Hollowford for the festival, winters at the ducal seat of Vareth-Hold. Twice married; widowed once. Remembered for his administrative caution and his patronage of the mendicants at Saint Perell. Appointments for his final audiences were kept by Scribe-Warden Tallow and should be retrievable.

### Lady Elayne Vareth
Twenty-nine. Aldric's elder daughter by his second marriage. Educated at the royal archives, currently holds no court office. Sharp, formal in public, dry in private. Has been at Greystone for the past two seasons organizing her father's papers. Confirmation as Duchess of Westvale is pending.

### Lord Edric Vareth
Thirty-four. Aldric's son by his first marriage. Commands a cavalry wing at Thornwatch. Not close to his father in the last decade. His claim is thinner than Elayne's under the charter but not dismissible; the Thornwatch branch of the family backs him.

### Lady Marrow Vareth
Aldric's surviving widow. Lives mostly at Greystone. Rarely seen at court since the funeral arrangements. Sister Maren reports she is grieving but lucid.

## Council of Stewards

### Steward-Principal Callum Orell
Late sixties. Has served under three dukes. Runs the council like a guildhouse: formal, procedural, allergic to surprise. Will be the single hardest obstacle or the single most valuable ally during the succession.

### Scribe-Warden Bess Tallow
Forties. Keeps the appointment logs, the granary ledgers, and most of the duchy's institutional memory. Knew Duke Aldric's daily movements better than his widow did. Currently cautious with the party but not hostile.

## Foreign Court

### Ambassador Suneth Vae
Fenland legation, about forty. Wears a silver pendant of her house; the make is similar but not identical to the one found at the scene. Polished, careful, multilingual. Her extended stay in Hollowford is the single most-discussed anomaly among the Silverflow Guild's gossipers.
