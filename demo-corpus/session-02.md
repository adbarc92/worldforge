# Session 2 — The Road North

**Date played:** real-world last Thursday
**In-world date:** 4th–12th of Harvest-Tide, 847
**Location:** Silverflow road, between Thornwatch and Hollowford
**Party:** Corrin, Vesh, Sister Maren, Halden, Old Tomas

## Summary

Ten days of travel compressed into one session of play. The party rode hard where the terrain allowed and moved carefully where it didn't. Two notable stops and one bad night on the river.

## The Ferry at Redstone Bend

The Silverflow is too wide to ford at Redstone, and the ferry there is operated by a Silverflow Guild concession. The guild master asked more questions than was polite — in particular he wanted to know whether the party was carrying anything "for the Hollowford residence of the duke." The party answered vaguely. He let them through but wrote in a ledger as they crossed.

Halden clocked at least two figures watching the crossing from the opposite bank. They did not follow on the road.

## The Village of Harren-on-Silverflow

Stopped overnight at the village inn on the 7th. Sister Maren visited the shrine of Saint Perell for the evening rite and spent an hour with the shrine-keeper, an old woman named Oelda.

Oelda remembered the duke's patronage from years past — the roof repairs he had funded, the bushel he sent each autumn. "He was a kind man," she said. "He always remembered us."

The party left her a small offering for the shrine in the morning.

## The Bad Night on the River

On the 10th, the party camped within sight of the Silverflow at a stretch called the Oxbow. Sometime after the third watch, Corrin heard oars on the water. A single small boat, no lantern, moving upstream against the current with two rowers. Too far to intercept, too obvious to ignore.

Nothing came of it that night, but the party agreed to take the remaining road in two watches instead of one.

## Arrival

The party reached the southern gate of Hollowford on the evening of the 12th of Harvest-Tide. The city was already decorated for the festival. Lodging was arranged through a contact of Sister Maren's at the Saint Perell hostel.

An audience at the ducal seat was requested for the 14th.

## Loose Threads

- The Silverflow Guild master's interest in their cargo.
- The watchers at Redstone.
- The boat at the Oxbow.
- Whoever rode ahead of them from Thornwatch has had ten days to arrive in Hollowford first.
