# Crew Manifest — Aegis Station (2412)

## Command

- **Commander Iresh Vale** — Station Director. Former xenolinguist, appointed 2409. Widely respected but rarely seen outside the command deck since the third anomaly event.
- **Executive Officer Maren Koval** — Day-to-day operations. Ex-Navy, thirty-year service record. Runs a tight watch rotation.
- **Chief Engineer Teodor Weiss** — Reactor and life support. Came up from the Hestia shipyards; known for fixing things nobody asked him to fix.

## Science Division

- **Dr. Anika Rho** — Lead researcher, Halion Anomaly Project. Authored the contested 2410 paper arguing the pulse is a structured transmission, not natural phenomenon.
- **Dr. Elias Fen** — Computational xenology. Responsible for the signal-analysis cluster in the central core.
- **Dr. Saoirse Lang** — Materials and propulsion. Technically assigned to propulsion research but has been pulled into anomaly work for the last eight months.

## Security

- **Captain Drest Hollow** — Head of Station Security. Reports directly to Commander Vale. Maintains a private comms channel to Commonwealth Defense.
- Twelve security personnel, rotating four per shift.

## Notable Civilians

- **Teague Marr** — Archivist. Civilian contractor. Unusually well-informed for someone whose nominal job is keeping the restricted archives organized.
- **Ori Sato** — Medical officer and only qualified surgeon on-station. Works alone.
