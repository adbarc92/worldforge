# The Kingdom of Valeria

Valeria is a mountainous kingdom ruled by Queen Seraphina. The capital city is Aurelis, built on the slopes of Mount Kaldor. The kingdom was founded in the year 1204 by King Aldric the Third.

The royal family descends from the line of Valdemar. They are known for their silver hair and emerald eyes. The royal guard wears blue cloaks embroidered with the kingdom's sigil: a golden hawk.
